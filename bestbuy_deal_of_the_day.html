Description

Premium all-in-one remote start system with two super-long range, 2-way LED remote transmitters. Capable of remote starting your vehicle from up to 6000-feet away. Bypass module included. Compatible with most vehicles 1988 and up (automatic transmission only).

Features

Professional installation is required for this product

Additional parts and labor are usually required. NOTE: cost of additional parts and labor typically ranges from $10 to $300, depending upon vehicle make and model - please contact your local Best Buy store for exact pricing.

Compatible with most vehicles with automatic transmission

Remote start almost any vehicle to warm up the inside in winter, or cool it down in summer.

Two-way LED remote with range up to 6000

Remote start your vehicle and receive instant confirmation from up to a mile away.

Remote start from the factory key-fob

Ask your installer about adding remote start capabilities to your factory key remote.

Bypass module included

The HCX control module included with the iDataStart HC3.5 has a built-in bypass module for seamless integration with your vehicle.

DroneMobile compatible

Add the Drone X1MAX-LTE for unlimited range smartphone control and GPS tracking with the DroneMobile App.

Compustar kit is a few years old but its cheaper on sale -- it has 3000ft max range and comes with one 2way remote and one 1way remote. iDataStart kit looks like a recently upgraded version of their old HC3 system but costs more -- it has 6000ft max range and comes with two 2way remotes. Get the Compustar on sale if you're on a budget or don't need extra range otherwise pay more for iDataStart on sale for longer range and a second 2way remote.
